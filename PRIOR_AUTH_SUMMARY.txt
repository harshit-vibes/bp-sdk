================================================================================
USE CASES
================================================================================

ROUTINE AUTHORIZATION (Simple)
- Time: ~3 minutes
- Input: Clear policy, recent records
- Output: Well-structured submission

COMPLEX AUTHORIZATION (Evidence gaps)
- Time: ~5 minutes
- Input: Complex policy, incomplete records
- Output: Submission with gap identification
- Action: Obtain missing docs, resubmit

URGENT AUTHORIZATION (Time-sensitive)
- Time: ~4 minutes (expedited)
- Input: Policy, clinical info, urgency flag
- Output: Expedited submission with urgent contacts
- Action: Fast payer follow-up
